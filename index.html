Monster of Monsters

This is a timeline of the notable moments of Godzilla's Life

  • 1954 - Godzilla is awakened/created from nuclear weapons testing in the Pacific
  • 1954 - Godzilla sinks numerous fishing/commercial vessels and destroys Tokyo for the first time
  • 1954 - Godzilla is destroyed by the "Oxygen-Destroyer" weapon created by Dr. Daisuke Serizawa
  • 1955 - Another Godzilla is discovered near Iwata Island, as well as another creature, "Anguirus". Both creatures make landfall at Soporo, Anguirus is killed. Godzilla is later entombed on an icy island somewhere near Hakaido
  • 1962 - King Kong is discovered on an island in the South Pacific. Godzilla is rediscovered after escaping his ice tomb. The two creatures make their way to Japan, eventually having a climactic battle on the slopes of Mt. Fuji. Kong is the apparent winner, Godzilla's fate is unknown
